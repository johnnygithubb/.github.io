Why Choose Door Hangers?

In a world flooded with digital noise, door hangers cut through the clutter. They are tangible, targeted, and impossible to ignore.

100% Visual Contact

Unlike an email that gets deleted unseen, a door hanger must be physically handled to be removed. This guarantees a 100% visual impression for your brand.

Superior Response Rates

According to the Direct Marketing Association (DMA), door-to-door marketing initiatives can achieve response rates as high as 3-5%, compared to just 1% for standard direct mail.

Hyper-Local Targeting

We cover Corpus Christi, Padre Island, Port Aransas, Aransas Pass, and Rockport. No wasted ad spend on people who live too far away to visit your business.

Cost-Effective ROI

With lower production costs than direct mail (no postage required) and higher engagement, the Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) is significantly higher.

Competitive Advantage

95% of your competition is fighting for attention in crowded social feeds and spam folders. By being on the front door, you stand alone without the noise.

Meet Our Founder

Johnathan Strawn, a dynamic entrepreneur and Corpus Christi local, relocated from Dallas where he spearheaded groundbreaking consulting projects, building powerhouse brands across diverse industries, pioneering innovative app designs, and masterminding marketing strategies that scaled companies to multimillion-dollar valuations. Recruited by a leading real estate investing firm in Corpus, he identified a critical pain point plaguing small business owners: pouring fortunes into ineffective marketing campaigns with zero ROI. Through rigorous testing and data-driven insights, John discovered that door hangers deliver unparalleled results—often outperforming digital ads by a wide margin. Yet, owners were hamstrung by the hassle of self-distribution or unreliable, overpriced labor. Spotting that competitors inflated distribution costs by an average of 40%, John founded Avenue Direct to revolutionize the game: providing premium, affordable door hanger solutions that guarantee efficient, trackable delivery. Today, Avenue Direct empowers small businesses in the cutthroat home services economy to thrive, turning marketing challenges into scalable success stories.
